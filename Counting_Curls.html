Counting curls is hard – let us do it for you!

Counting Curls is a fitness application platform that allows users to track their fitness workouts and graph their progress.
Counting Curls stores information as users input their fitness exercise, repetitions, and weight amounts.

Target Customer Groups

  • People trying to lose weight or gain muscle.
  • Regular fitness hobbyists trying to track their workouts.
  • Fitness gyms and supplement companies looking to advertise.
  • Anyone looking to track their workouts and fitness progress effectively.

FOR health enthusiasts WHO need a one-stop-shop for all things fitness, THE CountingCurls is a fitness
app THAT provides an easy way to track fitness goals and progress and receive personalized recommendations.
UNLIKE other fitness apps, OUR PRODUCT will provide reminder alerts and be offered to users at no cost.

Features

  • Could offer suggestions on supplements
  • Could you recommend gyms or trainers near you?
  • Could offer instructions on how to perform specific exercises
    and explain which muscles they affect the most
  • Could offer healthy nutrition diets
  • Could store workout exercises and routines
